About Me

Since then, I have been dedicated to building my knowledge in areas such as network security, threat analysis, and ethical hacking. I enjoy learning Linux, Python, and cloud infrastructure while continuing to build technical skills. I am committed to applying my skills and experience to enhance digital security and support individuals and organizations in protecting against evolving cyber threats.
